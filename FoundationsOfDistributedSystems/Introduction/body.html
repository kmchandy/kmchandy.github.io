Foundations Of Distributed Systems

This chapter describes the foundational concepts of distributed systems.

The module on agents and channels describes how distributed systems, in this course, are specified as directed graph in which vertices represent agents and edges represent channels. A computation of a distributed system is defined in terms of a nondeterministic loop described in the previous chapter.

The next module on timelines shows how the computation of a distributed system can be defined in terms of a timeline that shows the states of component agents and channels as time progresses.
