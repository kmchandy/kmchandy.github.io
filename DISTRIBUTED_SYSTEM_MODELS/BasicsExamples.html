Example of Channel States

The figure below shows a sequence of states S0, S1, S2, S3 of a channel from agent A to agent B. In state S0 the channel is empty, i.e., its state is the empty queue. When agent A sends message M1 the state of the channel changes to S1, a queue consisting of the single message M1. The states after A sends M2 and B receives a message are shown in the figure.
