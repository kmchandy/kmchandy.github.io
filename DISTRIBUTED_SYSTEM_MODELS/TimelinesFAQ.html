Timelines: FAQ

For two cuts (past, future) and (past', future'), is it the case that either past is a subset of past' or past' is a subset of past ?/p> No

In figure 1 there are cuts (past, future) and (past', future') in which past is {0, 1} and past' is {0, 2}.

For a cut (past, future) could there exist computations that execute some events in future before executing events in past?

yes

In figure 1 there is a cut (past, future) in which past is {0, 1} and [0, 2, 1, 3, 4] is a computation.

Is "every message received in past is sent in past" equivalent to "every message sent in future is received in future"?

Yes

Note that a message may be received in the final state.
