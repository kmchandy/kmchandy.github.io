Useful Formulae

Relationships between \(N\), \(A\), \(E\), \(\rightarrow\), \(\leadsto\)

  1. \( P \rightarrow Q \quad \equiv \quad P \Rightarrow N(Q) \)
  2. \( P \stackrel{*}{\rightarrow} Q \quad \equiv \quad P \Rightarrow A(Q) \)
  3. \( P \leadsto Q \quad \equiv \quad P \Rightarrow E(Q) \)

Monotonicity

  1. \( P \Rightarrow Q \quad \equiv \quad N(P) \Rightarrow N(Q) \)
  2. \( P \Rightarrow Q \quad \equiv \quad A(P) \Rightarrow A(Q) \)
  3. \( P \Rightarrow Q \quad \equiv \quad E(P) \Rightarrow E(Q) \)

Idempotence

  1. \( A(A(Q)) = Q \)
  2. \( E(E(Q)) = Q \)

Distributivity over Conjunction

  1. \( N(P \wedge Q) = N(P) \wedge N(Q) \)
  2. \( A(P \wedge Q) = A(P) \wedge A(Q) \)
  3. \( E(A(P \wedge Q)) = E(A(P)) \wedge E(A(Q)) \)

Stable Sets

The following are equivalent:
  1. \( stable(Q) \)
  2. \( Q \Rightarrow N(Q) \)
  3. \( Q \equiv A(Q) \)
  4. \( Q \rightarrow Q \)
  5. \( Q \stackrel{*}{\rightarrow} Q \)

Conjunctions and Disjunctions of Stable Predicates are Stable

  1. \( stable(P) \wedge stable(Q) \quad \equiv \quad stable(P \wedge Q) \)
  2. \( stable(P) \vee stable(Q) \quad \equiv \quad stable(P \vee Q) \)

Corollary

The stable sets that are subsets of \(Q\) form a lattice with the empty set at the bottom and \(A(Q)\) at the top.

Stability of Always

  1. \( stable(A(Q)) \)
  2. \( stable(E(A(Q))) \)

Single Hops and Infinite Paths

  1. \( A(Q) = Q \wedge N(A(Q)) \)
  2. \( E(Q) = Q \vee N(E(Q)) \)

Properties similar to those of Implication

Strengthen Left-Hand Side

  1. \( (R \Rightarrow P) \wedge (P \Rightarrow Q) \quad \Rightarrow \quad (R \Rightarrow Q) \)
  2. \( (R \Rightarrow P) \wedge \{P\}c\{Q\} \quad \Rightarrow \quad \{R\}c\{Q\} \)
  3. \( (R \Rightarrow P) \wedge P \rightarrow Q \quad \Rightarrow \quad R \rightarrow Q \)
  4. \( (R \Rightarrow P) \wedge P \leadsto Q \quad \Rightarrow \quad R \leadsto Q \)

Weaken Right-Hand Side

  1. \( (Q \Rightarrow R) \wedge (P \Rightarrow Q) \quad \Rightarrow \quad (P \Rightarrow R) \)
  2. \( (Q \Rightarrow R) \wedge \{P\}c\{Q\} \quad \Rightarrow \quad \{P\}c\{R\} \)
  3. \( (Q \Rightarrow R) \wedge P \rightarrow Q \quad \Rightarrow \quad P \rightarrow R \)
  4. \( (Q \Rightarrow R) \wedge P \leadsto Q \quad \Rightarrow \quad P \leadsto R \)

Disjunction Left-Hand Side

  1. \( (P \Rightarrow R) \wedge (Q \Rightarrow R) \quad \equiv \quad ((P \vee Q) \Rightarrow R) \)
  2. \( \{P\}c\{R\} \wedge \{Q\}c\{R\} \quad \equiv \quad \{P \vee Q\}c\{R\} \)
  3. \( (P \rightarrow R) \wedge (Q \rightarrow R) \quad \equiv \quad (P \vee Q) \rightarrow R \)
  4. \( (P \leadsto R) \wedge (Q \leadsto R) \quad \equiv \quad ((P \vee Q) \leadsto R) \)

Conjunction Right-Hand Side

  1. \( (P \Rightarrow Q) \wedge (P \Rightarrow R) \quad \equiv \quad P \Rightarrow (Q \wedge R) \)
  2. \( \{P\}c\{Q\} \wedge \{P\}c\{R\} \quad \equiv \quad \{P\}c\{Q \wedge R\} \)
  3. \( (P \rightarrow Q) \wedge (P \rightarrow R) \quad \equiv \quad P \rightarrow (Q \wedge R) \)
  4. \( (P \leadsto A(Q)) \wedge (P \leadsto A(R)) \quad \equiv \quad P \leadsto A(Q \wedge R) \)
